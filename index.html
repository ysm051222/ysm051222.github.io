I am an aspiring linguistics student with interests spanning several subfields of linguistics.

As my understanding of the field continues to develop, I am actively exploring a wide range of topics in linguistics. I am especially drawn to the intersections of linguistic structure, mathematical form, cognition, sound, and data. More specifically, I am interested in how the human-specific capacity for language emerged. This has led me to explore the biological, psychological, and mathematical factors that may have contributed to the emergence of the language faculty.
